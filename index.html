Background

I'm a child of the southeast. I've got a few bad habits but I try to treat people well and take care of my family. I've grown up in the movies and as an adult started FilmDispenser.com; a community for like-minded pop culture enthusiasts. Podcasting became a way of life but filmmaking was the goal and web design a byproduct. I've put the mic down and picked up a camera. My first short, Say Hello played at the 2016 Chattanooga Film Festival and the first Way Down Short Film Festival in Columbus, GA. I'm working on my fourth short now and a new career in web design, and I'm most excited for what is to come from finally putting my heart into all of the work I do.

I'm married to a beautiful photographer, Katy Howard. We have one son and he's a little over a year old. I named him Ezra after the Star Wars Rebels character, which should fill in a few blanks about myself. My years at FilmDispenser.com have lead me to chase all of my passions. Come on this journey with me, won't you?
